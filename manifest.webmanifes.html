About EASE IN Hospice Care, Inc.

Our Mission

We help families navigate hospice care options with compassion, clarity, and respect. Our team provides timely information and coordination so loved ones can focus on what matters most.

Our Approach

Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 18, 2025

Overview

EASE IN Hospice Care, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates this informational website. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what limited data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you voluntarily send via email or phone when you reach out (e.g., name, email, phone, and any details you include in your message).
  • Basic site analytics if enabled (e.g., aggregated traffic counts). We do not sell personal information.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information.
  • To improve our website and services.

Cookies & Third‑Party Services

This site may use lightweight analytics or embedded content (e.g., Google Maps). These services may set their own cookies. Refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information only when required by law or to protect our rights.
