What is hospice care?

Hospice care is specialized support for people living with a life-limiting illness. The focus is on comfort and quality of life ‚Äî not cure ‚Äî so that patients and families can experience as much peace as possible, often in the comfort of home.

Hospice teams work together to provide:

  • Pain and symptom management
  • Emotional and spiritual support
  • Guidance for families during difficult decisions
  • Education so families feel prepared and supported
  • 24/7 help for urgent questions or changes
